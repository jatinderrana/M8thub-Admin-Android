User Content

User Content uploaded to M8thub servers are flagged into two categories:

  1. Permitted Content
  2. Banned COntent

Permitted Content

Permitted content are materials that fall outside the list of "Banned Content".

Banned Content

Banned content are materials that falls within the list of restricted materials which includes the following:

  • Materials, both static and animated, that shows copyrighted content like movies, television programs, advertisements and video clips that belongs to an individual, group or company. Permission is required to show an allowable portion of the material and a proper label showing the original owners of the copyrighted content.
  • Materials, both static and animated, that shows graphic sexual content and nudity including pornographic visuals.
  • Materials, both static and animated, that promotes racism, hate and extremely violent visuals.
  • Materials, both static and animated, that shows prolonged graphic or sadistic realistic violence.
  • Materials, both static and animated, that shows and promotes false news or information unless the content is labeled as "satire".

Exemptions to Banned Content:

These materials falls under the common "Freedom of Speech" and will not be flagged as "Banned Content".

  • Materials, both static and animated, that shows unproven conspiracy theories.

  • Materials, both static and animated, that shows pranks, emotionally provoking visuals and jokes.
  • Materials, both static and animated, that debunks existing and non-existing ideas, theories and concepts.
